About KnowledgeHub

Empowering learners through interactive assessment and skill development

Our Mission

At KnowledgeHub, we believe that assessment is the first step towards mastery. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, engaging, and accessible platform for learners worldwide to test their knowledge, track progress, and achieve their educational goals.

We combine cutting-edge technology with pedagogical expertise to create tests that are not just assessments, but learning experiences in themselves.

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, KnowledgeHub started as a small project by a group of educators and technologists. Today, we serve over 50,000 learners across 120 countries, offering more than 150 specialized tests across various domains.
