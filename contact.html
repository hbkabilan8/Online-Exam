Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, please check your spam folder.

Are the tests timed?

Yes, most tests have a time limit which is displayed before you start. However, we offer some untimed practice tests as well. You can check the specific test details on the test information page.

Can I retake a test?

Yes, you can retake most tests. However, your previous scores will be saved in your history. Some certification tests may have restrictions on retakes within a certain time period.

Do you offer certificates?

Yes, we offer digital certificates for certification tests upon achieving a passing score. These certificates can be downloaded and shared on professional networks like LinkedIn.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
